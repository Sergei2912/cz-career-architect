What is a cover letter, and why is it important?
A cover letter is a personalized document that accompanies your resume when applying for physician positions. Unlike a letter of intent, which is typically used when two parties want to outline the key terms of a potential agreement before entering into a formal contract, a cover letter addresses specific job openings. It highlights how your qualifications and past experiences align with the employer’s current requirements. The proper
cover letter format
